About Punyah Yoga

At Punyah Yoga, we welcome seekers from around the world to experience the ancient wisdom of Yoga at its very source – Rishikesh, the Yoga Capital of the World. Our teachings go beyond physical postures, helping you unlock true mental and spiritual transformation.

Through personalized training, experienced instructors, and a holistic approach, we aim to provide a path to self-discovery, balance, and inner peace.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a sacred and welcoming space where individuals can dive deep into the essence of yoga, both physically and spiritually.

Our certified, passionate teachers ensure every student gets personal guidance to master yoga asanas, philosophy, and lifestyle — not just for teaching, but for self-transformation.

Whether you're seeking certification or self-growth, our school offers a journey into meditation, Karma Yoga, Yoga Nidra, Ayurveda, and the beauty of Rishikesh.

Wholesome Food with Ayurvedic Essence

Our meals are freshly prepared by our in-house Ayurvedic chef, using non-GMO, chemical-free ingredients that nourish both the body and mind.

Enjoy delicious vegetarian and vegan meals, refreshing coconut water, seasonal fruits, and herbal teas – crafted to keep you energized for every yoga session.

We believe food is a part of spiritual discipline – and every bite reflects purity, love, and health.

Comfortable Accommodation

Stay at our luxurious Punyah Residency — equipped with rooftop yoga shala, garden terrace, and all modern facilities.

Rooms include 24/7 hot water, high-speed Wi-Fi, balconies with mountain views, and are available on a single or shared basis.

Located in peaceful Tapovan near the Ganges, it’s an ideal place to unwind after your yoga sessions.
