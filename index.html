Welcome to Punyah Yoga

Internationally Certified Yoga Teacher Training School In India

Punyah Yoga in Rishikesh offers authentic Yoga Teacher Training Courses (TTC) designed to awaken your inner strength and spiritual growth. From 100-hour, 200-hour, to 300-hour Yoga TTC, we deliver transformative experiences led by certified instructors in the serene Himalayan foothills.
Alliance-certified teacher training Course while experiencing the true ashram lifestyle. Connect with a mindful community, deepen your yoga practice, and find spiritual retreat in the serene Himalayas. Embrace tradition, connection, and holistic wellness at our transformative yoga school. Join us for a journey to enlightenment and mindful living.

Learn More

Choosing the right yoga teacher training in India — especially in Rishikesh — can feel overwhelming. That’s exactly why we created Punyah Yoga: a space built on authenticity, accessibility, and international standards.

Nestled in the spiritual heart of Rishikesh, the yoga capital of the world, we offer globally accredited courses that prepare you to teach with confidence — anywhere.

  • Deep roots: Emphasis on ancient yoga philosophy, not just poses
  • Approachable teaching: Instructors who adapt for every body type
  • Supportive vibe: A warm, family-like learning environment

This isn’t just a certificate — it’s a full transformation of body, mind, and soul.

Led by 5 experienced teachers, your training journey includes:

  • Ashtanga, Hatha Yoga, Alignment & Adjustments
  • Ayurveda, Pranayama, Meditation, Mantras
  • Yoga Philosophy, History & Sanskrit insights

You’ll master sequencing, posture modifications, online + offline class strategies, and dive into the sacred depth of the eight limbs of yoga.

  • Interactive & Immersive: More than a course — it’s a family-like atmosphere with deep, spiritual immersion.
  • Teaching to Teach: We guide you to confidently lead your own classes — or even open your yoga studio.
  • Beyond Asanas: Dive into the psychology, philosophy, and true purpose of yoga — far beyond just poses.
  • Deeper Connection: It’s your personal journey. Grow spiritually as you align body, breath, and soul.
